Privacy Policy

RetireSmart  ·  Last updated: June 2026

RetireSmart ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly what data the app collects, why, how it is stored, and your rights over it.

Short version: We collect only what is necessary to run the app — your email for sign-in, your financial data for cloud sync, and screenshots only when you choose to import them. We never sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except the services listed below.

1. Who We Are

RetireSmart is an Android personal finance and retirement planning application published on Google Play by The Finance Sanctuary. You can contact us at thefinancesanctuary@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

Data Why we collect it Where it is stored

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your precise or approximate location
  • We do not collect your name (only your email)
  • We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry beyond what is inherent to Supabase auth
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or ad networks
  • We do not sell your personal data — ever

4. Camera and Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your camera and photo library only when you choose to import a holdings screenshot. Specifically:

  • Camera — lets you photograph a broker statement or holdings page directly
  • Photo library — lets you select an existing screenshot from your gallery

When an image is submitted for import, it is transmitted over HTTPS to our secure server-side proxy, which forwards it to Google Gemini Vision to extract ticker symbols and share counts. The image is not stored on our servers or in any database after processing is complete. You can use every other feature of the app without ever granting camera or photo access.

5. In-App Purchases

RetireSmart offers optional Pro and Family subscriptions and a lifetime purchase, processed through Google Play Billing via RevenueCat. Google handles all payment processing. We do not store your credit card details or payment information. Your subscription status is verified through RevenueCat using a unique anonymous identifier tied to your Google Play account.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

7. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and Supabase is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest. Screenshot images submitted for AI import are transmitted over HTTPS and are not retained after processing. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

8. Data Retention and Account Deletion

Your account and financial data remain in our cloud database (Supabase) for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time using either method below:

  • In-app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This immediately and permanently removes all your data from our servers.
  • By email: Email thefinancesanctuary@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.
  • You may sign out of the app at any time via Portfolio → Sign out
  • Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your cloud data — use one of the deletion methods above to remove your data

9. Children's Privacy

RetireSmart is intended for users aged 13 and older. Users aged 13–17 may use the app with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The "child profile" feature within the app is a planning tool for parents and guardians to model education savings goals — it does not collect any data from or about minors, and all data associated with child profiles is entered by the parent or guardian.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data through the app, please contact us at thefinancesanctuary@gmail.com and we will remove it promptly.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data (see Section 8)
  • Portability — receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email thefinancesanctuary@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and may notify you via an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
